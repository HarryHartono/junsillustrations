About Me

Hi, I am Jun from Penang, Malaysia. I hold a Master’s degree in Conservation of Fine Arts, with a specialisation in easel painting. My passion lies in the preservation and restoration of artworks, and my academic training has equipped me with a strong foundation in both the technical and scientific aspects of art conservation.

I am now seeking opportunities to apply my knowledge and hands-on experience in a professional setting. Dedicated to continual learning and growth, I am especially committed to advancing my practice in the field of easel painting conservation.
