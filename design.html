24 Solar Terms

A whimsical journey through the traditional Chinese calendar, illustrated through the eyes of a charming cow. This postcard collection brings to life the ancient wisdom of the 24 solar terms, each capturing the subtle changes in nature throughout the year.

The series combines traditional Chinese cultural elements with contemporary design, creating a bridge between past and present. Each illustration tells a story of seasonal transformation, agricultural wisdom, and natural phenomena, making these complex concepts accessible and engaging.

Astronaut Gift Collection

An imaginative stationery collection that takes you on a journey beyond the stars. This complete gift set was crafted as an entry for UK Greetings, showcasing a playful blend of cosmic wonder and practical design.

While this project didn't secure a win in the competition, it represents a creative exploration of space-themed design, featuring a cohesive collection of greeting cards, notepads, and gift packaging. The design captures the whimsy of space exploration while maintaining a sophisticated and commercially viable aesthetic.
